About Orpita Studio

We build new worlds, one story at a time.

Who We Are

Orpita is an independent game studio based in Alexandria, Egypt. Founded in May 2025, we specialize in creating high-concept interactive experiences that push the boundaries of indie gaming. Our team is driven by a singular goal: to build immersive, logic-defying worlds that empower players to explore the extraordinary.

Our Philosophy

Our values guide every world we build and every story we tell.

Focus your mind

In an era of brain-rot distractions, Orpita crafts experiences that reclaim your attention and reward your intellectual curiosity.

Imagine your world

Explore realms where beauty meets purpose, crafted by dreamers who believe that every game should be a breathtaking piece of living art.

Raise your limits

Driven by an insatiable hunger for excellence, we break through every ceiling to deliver experiences that grow bolder and more ambitious with every release.

A Word From Our Founder

Look for inspiration, lead the vision.

"Many games recently have started relying on tricks to make players come back, rather than focusing on solid game mechanics or good design. That’s what we wanted to change with Orpita, a studio where a team of designers, artists, and of course programmers work together to create fun games without mind tricks or manipulating players."

- Amr Elmahdy
